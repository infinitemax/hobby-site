About me

Climber (and developer?)

Hi, I'm Max

Thanks for visiting this website, about one of my favourite hobbies: climbing! I picked it up about 7 years ago and, like many who try it, was addicted straight away. Not only is it a great way to get fit, it also comes with a community of really nice people!

FAM

(Facts about me)

Name

My name is long - Maxwell Fionn Brookman-Byrne. It can be annoying filling out forms...

Location

I currently live in Sheffield but grew up on the south coast, and have lived in Guildford, Brussels, London, and Lincoln.

Career so far

Until recently I was a senior lecturer in Law, teaching and researching in human rights and focusing on the use of armed drones in conflict. But I'm seeking a change so have taken a leap to retrain as a developer.

What I like to do

Clearly I'm into climbing, but I also enjoy surfing, cycle touring, and playing music. I can't live without a social life and really enjoy being around good people.
