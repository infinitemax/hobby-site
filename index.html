The Peak District

Home of climbing in the UK

The Peak District National Park is the birthplace of climing in the UK. It is home to thousands of climbs of various styles, offering enough variety to last a lifetime.

Types of climbing in the PDNP

The PDNP is host to numerous venues for many types of climbing. The most common is trad, where the climber protects themselves as they ascend the rock.

Other common styles are bouldering (a very physically challenging form of climbing on large boulders), and sport climbing, where protective bolts are fixed to the rock in advance.
